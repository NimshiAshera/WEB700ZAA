Program Overview

  1. When you graduate you will be equipped to help shape how businesses manage their connected systems.
  2. This program is offered in Seneca's hybrid delivery format with some courses available in Seneca's flexible delivery format.
  3. Career Pathway
    • technical support specialist
    • helpdesk support
    • network systems administrator
  4. This program includes an optional work term where you can put your skills into practice and gain valuable work experience in the IT industry.
  5. Students meeting all academic requirements may have the opportunity to complete an optional work term(s) in a formal work environment.
